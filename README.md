## ⚙️ Advanced Anti-Manipulation (Sybil Resistance)

Maintaining a fair and un-gameable leaderboard is the foundation of our trust. The bot utilizes a multi-layered defense mechanism against fake votes:

1. **Mandatory Channel Verification:** Users cannot cast a vote unless the bot verifies their active membership in the official [Drokia Trends Network](https://t.me/DrokiaTrends). This forces users to be part of the broader ecosystem.
2. **Strict Time-Lock Cooldowns:** A mathematically precise **1-hour cooldown** is applied per user, per project. The bot calculates the exact time remaining and dynamically informs the user with a custom visual warning.
3. **Rolling 12-Hour Active Window:** The global leaderboard is never static. It constantly filters out historical data, only counting votes cast within the **last 12 hours**. This guarantees that the trending list reflects current, explosive momentum rather than past glory.
